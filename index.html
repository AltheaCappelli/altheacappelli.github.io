Hi, I'm Althea,
and welcome to my website!

I’m passionate about programming, solving complex problems, and contributing to a more sustainable future; here I share personal projects I’ve worked on out of curiosity and to further develop my coding skills.

I studied physics at Leibniz University Hannover, where I focused on theoretical physics during my Master’s and spent a semester abroad in Helsinki.
In my Bachelor’s thesis, I explored the probability of recovering quantum information from a black hole using tensor networks. For my Master’s, I modeled a proposed dark matter detection experiment and simulated its response to a theoretical signal using numerical methods.

Alongside my studies, I took extra lectures in computer physics and data science in Python, and have since deepened my skills in software development, data analysis and machine learning. I also picked up HTML, CSS, and SQL to build this website and support my projects.

I speak Italian, German, and English fluently, plus Spanish and a bit of Finnish. Outside of work, you’ll likely find me bouldering, training taekwondo, or out hiking somewhere in nature :)
